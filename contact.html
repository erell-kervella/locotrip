We are four students in IESEG working for the course Digital Technology who decided to create a website to compete against Airbnb, Bookings, etc. Our main differentiator is that not only you are going to choose where are you staying in the place you want to visit but also with WHO and WHAT are you going to do. We belive that a good vacation is more than finding the right place to stay, it is also about having a good company where you can learn a lot about a culture and a place and also do experiences not available without the presence of a local person.

LocoTrips try to combine those three elements giving a recipe of:

PLACE + HOST + EXPERIENCE = FUN
