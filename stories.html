Trip to Oaxaca,Jonah

Whether you're into art, architecture, food, history, spirits of the drinking kind, or handicrafts,

Oaxaca has it in spades. "One of the most delightful cities I've ever visited," wrote one World's

Best voter. A center for mole and mezcal making, Oaxaca is filled with fantastic markets, including

Mercado Benito Juárez, where visitors can find local staples like grasshopper tacos.

The city also boasts both a modern art museum in a 17th-century mansion and, 20 minutes away,

an impressive pre-Colombian archaeological site. Artisanal culture is not just alive in this

southern Mexican city but thriving. "We visited a basket maker, a lady making red pottery, rug

weavers who dye their own yarn, and an amazing group of woodcarvers that paint/decorate the

carving."
