Most loved hosts

Mr. Clement Cohen


Clement will take you into his home to make you discover Paris, the french gastronomy and some secret spots in the city to take the best couple photos.

Lana Stone


Lana will show you around New York Like you have never seen before. Especially the masterpieces of arts that you can find in the MOMA museum or american theatres.

Anika and Hans know everything there is to know about Amsterdam for the tourists. Eat at the trendy restaurants of Europe, visit the parks and canals, appreciate modern art, etc. You will not be dissapointed by the guides !

Loic has lived all his life in the Canary Islands, he will be the most indicated to learn you how and where to surf. After 4 years as a surf instructor, he is able to teach to both begginers and advanced people. If you are willing to do watersports, this is the trip to book !
